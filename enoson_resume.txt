POSITION SUMMARY
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Tester (VAPT)
to join our cybersecurity team. As a VAPT expert, you will play a key role in identifying vulnerabilities,
performing penetration tests, and ensuring the overall security posture of our systems, applications, and
networks. Your work will help protect critical business data, prevent security breaches, and strengthen the
company’s security framework. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of cybersecurity
vulnerabilities, hacking methodologies, and penetration testing tools, with the ability to assess and manage
potential risks to organizational assets.

RESPONSIBILITES
Vulnerability Assessment
•

Conduct regular vulnerability scans on systems, networks, and applications to identify security
flaws.

•

Analyse and prioritize vulnerabilities based on risk and impact, providing detailed reports with
actionable recommendations for remediation.

•

Perform threat modelling and risk analysis to predict and mitigate security risks.

•

Collaborate with development and operations teams to ensure vulnerabilities are resolved
promptly.
Penetration Testing

•

Plan, execute, and document penetration tests on web applications, network infrastructure, and
other technologies to evaluate security weaknesses.

•

Use a variety of tools and techniques (e.g., Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nessus, Nmap) to simulate
real-world attacks and exploits.

•

Develop exploitation strategies and techniques for bypassing security controls and gaining
unauthorized access.

•

Provide detailed findings, including proof-of-concept exploitations, and suggest remediation
actions.
Security Audits & Compliance

•

Assist in security audits and assessments to ensure compliance with internal and external security
standards, including GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS.

•

Create and maintain security documentation for audits, risk assessments, and reporting purposes.
